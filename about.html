About Us

We dream of Education system where teachers teach, not by giving answers.

But they teach by asking questions. Because every child is different and only questions can make them express their uniqueness. Questions that make the child look deep into the wonders of the universe, and into themselves.

  • Questions make them think.
  • Questions make them answer.
  • Questions make them know the unknown facts.

Because, no child deserves education that conditions.
Only when I stand up for the child, My Teachers will, My Parents will.
When I stand up for the child, My classrooms change, My school will change.
When I stand up for the child, more schools will.
When schools stand for the child,
An education system for Human Potential will rise,
A new human being will rise.
